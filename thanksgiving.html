My family does not celebrate thanksgiving with turkeys.
Instead, we make dumplings.

Recipe


1. Make the dough
2. Make the filling
3. Flatten dough into small round circles
4. Put filling on dough
5. Wrap dough around filling
6. Boil the dumplings

Sometimes we make noodles as well.
