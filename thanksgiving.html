We celebrated Thanksgiving with our friends at my friend's house. So we didn't have turkey at home, instead we had dumplings.

Recipe
  • 1. Make the dough
  • 2. Make the filling
  • 3. Flatten dough into small round circles
  • 4. Put filling on dough
  • 5. Wrap dough around filling
  • 6. Boil the dumplings

Sometimes we make noodles as well.
